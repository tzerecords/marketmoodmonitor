### Data Strategy: Cache-First
**Why:**
- Avoid rate limits (CoinGecko: 30 calls/min)
- Faster load times (no wait for API)
- Resilience (fallback if API down)

**Implementation:**

Pseudo-logic

def get_market_data():
cache = load_from_sqlite()
if cache.is_fresh(ttl=10*60): # 10 min TTL
return cache.data
else:
fresh_data = fetch_from_apis()
save_to_sqlite(fresh_data)
return fresh_data

text

### Risk Score Formula: Multi-Factor Model
**Weights Rationale:**
- **Fear & Greed (35%)**: Most established sentiment metric
- **BTC Momentum (25%)**: BTC drives market direction
- **Volume Health (20%)**: Confirms conviction
- **Market Breadth (20%)**: Ensures rally isn't isolated

**Alternative considered**: Machine learning model
- **Rejected**: Overly complex for MVP, less transparent to users

## How Risk Score Works

The Market Mood Monitor aggregates multiple signals into a single 0-100 score:

**Formula:**
Risk Score = (Fear & Greed √ó 35%) + (BTC Momentum √ó 25%) + 
             (Volume Health √ó 20%) + (Market Breadth √ó 20%)

**Components:**

üß† **Fear & Greed Index (35%)**
Measures overall market sentiment based on volatility, volume, social media, surveys, and dominance metrics.
Source: Alternative.me

üìà **BTC Momentum (25%)**
Tracks Bitcoin's short-term trend using Rate of Change (7d vs 30d moving average).
Why: BTC typically leads the market.

üìä **Volume Health (20%)**
Compares current 24h volume to 7-day average.
Why: High volume confirms price moves are sustainable.

üåê **Market Breadth (20%)**
Percentage of top 100 cryptocurrencies with positive 24h performance.
Why: Broad participation indicates healthier rallies.

**Interpretation:**
- 0-30: Extreme Risk Off (defensive positioning)
- 31-60: Neutral (wait for clearer signals)
- 61-100: Risk On (constructive for exposure)

**Update Frequency:** Every 10 minutes | Manual refresh available

**Data Sources:** CoinGecko API (prices, volume), Alternative.me (F&G Index)

**Limitations:** Score reflects current state, not future prediction. Use as one input among many for investment decisions.
